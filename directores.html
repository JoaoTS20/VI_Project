Spider chart - Director's Specialty in Movie Genders

A radar or spider or web chart is a two-dimensional chart type designed to plot one or more series of values over multiple quantitative variables. In this case it will plot the percentage of Movies of a certain genre from a Director, being possible to check which genres of Movies the Director directs the most.
It also allows a direct comparison between directors.
