ScatterPlot - Budget By Popularity in Movies

The Scatterplot displays the relationship between the Budget of a Movie and the Popularity Score of a Movie. For each data point (Movie), the value of its Popularity Score is represented on the X axis, the Budget on the Y axis.

It is possible to filter by Production Company so that only Movie points from the searched Production Company appear. To reset, search by nothing (empthy search bar).

Tree Map - Languages/Gender by Movie Revenue

The Hierarchical Tree Map shows the relationship between the original Language of a Movie and it's revenue, by making showing via squared representations the percentage of revenue each language has for all movies in the dataset.
By clicking on a determined language the tree map descends a level below and shows the a Tree Map of Movie Gender by Revenue for films of said determined language.
By hovering over a square in both levels it's possible to see how much exact revenue it represents.

Stacked Area Chart - Evolution of Movies along time by Language and Production Company

The Stacked Area Chart displays how the number of Movies produced worlwide evolved by using either its original Language or its Production Companies.
It's possible to vizualizing this evolution by either entering the languages to compare or the name of the production companies.
